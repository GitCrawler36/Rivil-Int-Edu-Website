Our story

It began with a family and a simple idea

In 2004, a Sri Lankan family founded Rivil International Education Consultants with a simple conviction: every Sri Lankan student with the ambition to study abroad deserves honest, expert guidance — without having to pay for it.

More than 21 years later, that conviction has not changed. From our office on Galle Road in Mount Lavinia, our counsellors have guided students into universities across New Zealand, the United Kingdom, Australia, Canada, Malaysia and Singapore.

We remain a family business at heart. That means you are never a case number to us — you are a student with a goal, and our job is to help you reach it.
